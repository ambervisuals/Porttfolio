Dell Bluetooth Travel Mouse - MS700

This project transcends the boundaries of traditional product visualization — it’s a tribute to the elegance of tech aesthetics. I focused on the interplay of lighting and shading to transform the Dell Bluetooth Travel Mouse into more than just a peripheral; it becomes a visual statement that bridges the digital and physical worlds.

While certain color variants remain under wraps due to privacy and release constraints, the showcased imagery reflects the core intent: to elevate minimal industrial design through refined visual storytelling and artistic precision.
