The Accountant 2 – Previz, Look Development & Visuals

I was responsible for handling the visual aspects of The Accountant 2, including previs and look development. My work helped establish the visual tone early in production, guiding how key scenes would unfold in terms of mood, composition, and camera flow.

In addition to previs, I contributed to look development by crafting shaders, lighting setups, and visual styles that aligned with the film’s grounded yet cinematic aesthetic. This role was vital in shaping a cohesive and visually engaging narrative.
