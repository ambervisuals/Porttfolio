Animixies – Character Development

I contributed to character development for the Animixies movie, focusing on shaping distinctive and visually engaging characters that align with the film’s creative vision. My work involved refining character designs, expressions, and form to support storytelling and emotional depth.

By blending artistic direction with technical execution, I helped bring the characters to life in a way that resonated with the film’s tone and audience. This work played a key role in building a memorable and cohesive visual narrative.
