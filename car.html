Kathakali Dance

Hi everyone! I created this character over the last 3 months. It had certainly been a challenge, but I learned a ton and had a lot of fun making this character. Kathakali is a major form of classical Indian dance. It is a "story play" genre of art, but one distinguished by the elaborately colorful make-up and costumes of the traditional male actor-dancers. It is native to the Malayalam-speaking southwestern region of Kerala and is almost entirely practiced and appreciated by Malayali people. So I took the reference and tried to do something the same.
