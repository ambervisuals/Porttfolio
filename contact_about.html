I'm a CG artist with a strong foundation in photography and a genuine love for storytelling through light. Whether it's shaping the mood of a scene or bringing out the texture in a product, I combine artistic intuition with technical precision to create visuals that feel both immersive and meaningful. Over the years, I've had the chance to work on high-profile projects that pushed me to grow, explore, and innovate with every frame.

I’m drawn to warm colors, expressive language, and the little details that spark big ideas. When I’m not behind the screen or camera, you’ll probably find me sketching, daydreaming, or rambling about something oddly specific—usually rocks. It’s all part of how I see and build the worlds I imagine.
