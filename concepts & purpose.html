Lenovo ThinkCentre neo Ultra

This is my submission to Agora Studios AnimChallenge, November 2022, themed "Arcane". I took a few artistic liberties with the theme and animated Taliyah being ambushed in her journey through the Freljord back to Shurima, as if it was Arcane.

The models and the scenery are quite old and janky. In fact, I made them shortly after the release of Arcane season 1, inspired by it, and I thought this was a great time to bring them to life, finally.
