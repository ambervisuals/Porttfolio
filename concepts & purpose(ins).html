The Instigators – CG Artist

I worked as a CG Artist on The Instigators, contributing to the film’s visual storytelling through high-quality computer-generated assets. My role involved creating and refining 3D elements that seamlessly integrated into the live-action footage, enhancing both realism and cinematic impact.

With a focus on detail, consistency, and style, I collaborated closely with the team to maintain the film’s visual tone. This work supported key scenes and helped bring the director’s vision to life with technical precision and artistic finesse.
